01 About

Researching intelligent models for real-world time series.

I am currently pursuing my final-year Ph.D. in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, where I began my program in the fall of 2021. I am fortunate to be under the supervision of Prof. Duanqing Xu and Prof. Yabo Dong. Moreover, I am currently visiting the College of Computing and Data Science at Nanyang Technological University, under the supervision of Prof. Han Yu (since December 2024).

My primary research interests lie in sensor-based time-series data mining (e.g., EEG, ECG, and human activity recognition), with a focus on developing large language models (LLMs) for time-series analysis, generative modeling, federated learning, and transfer learning.
